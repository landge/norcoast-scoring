4 White Matter Changes (WMC) Rating Scale

To visually evaluate the amount of white matter changes we will use the solid four-step Fazekas scale. Where

0 equals No or only 1 WML

1 equals punctuate smal WMLs and can be considered normal even below the age of 65

2 equals early confluent WMLs and is considered abnormal under the age of 70

3 equals confluent WMLs and is always abnormal, poor outcome in terms of cognition and survival
